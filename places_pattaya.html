Places to visit in Pattaya

1

Sanctuary of Truth:

The Sanctuary of Truth is a gigantic all wood construction museum and one of the best places to visit in Pattaya. It is located at the Rachvate cape of Naklua Pattaya City. It is one and only all-wood sculpture building in the whole world.

You should visit this picturesque and scenic place once in your lifetime. You’ll see several local plays, martial arts, Thai dances, sword fighting, Thai boxing, etc. at this place.

Take lovely pictures at the photo points and make memories last forever. Enjoy their delightful food. The greenery and the overwhelming background of The Sanctuary of Truth Museum will be a lifetime experience for both of you.

2

Big Buddha:

The Big Buddha Statue in Pattaya is a cultural highlight. At Big Buddha Hill/ Wat Phra Yai, you can see the biggest Buddha statue of Chonburi province, which is visible from a distance even before you reach your destination.

It is located on top of Pratamnak Hill, south of the city of Pattaya. The enormous golden-colored Buddha is around 18 meters high and is officially named Phra Buddha Sukhothai Walai Chonlathan, and is also known as Luang Pho Yai.

3

Viewpoint in Pattaya:

Khao Pattaya Viewpoint is a small hilltop that offers spectacular panoramic views of the city. The sacred wat Kho Phra is the temple located at the hill and is also one of the best tourist attractions.

4

Sri Racha Tiger Zoo:

The Sri Racha Tiger Zoo is one of the best attractions in Pattaya. It is the biggest zoo of its kind in the entire world. Set in a lovely natural atmosphere and located on the outskirts of Pattaya, the zoo boasts of the largest number of tigers in Thailand.

You can see a huge crocodile population which makes it the largest crocodile zoo in the world. This zoo is a popular choice among everyone and is often visited by families for a one-of-its-kind experience.

You will see more than 400 Royal Bengal tigers freely roaming in their natural habitat

Opening hours: 08.00 AM to 06.00 PM
