Best Things to do in Pattaya with your family

Overview

Traveling to Pattaya with your wife? Explore some exotic places in Pattaya and try different activities during your trip. There are many exciting and interesting places to visit in Pattaya that will leave you speechless. Explore the scenic beauty of Pattaya along with other adventurous places you might visit. You’ll see some incredibly beautiful places in Pattaya with your loved one.

1

Shopping in Pattaya:

Several malls, markets, and stalls make the city the best shopping options for tourists. You’ll find an impressive collection of malls catering to a range of budgets.

Adventurous & Watersports activities:

Adventurous: Parasailing, Jet skiing, Bungee jump, Real gun shooting & Human Slingshot

Watersports: Snorkeling, Scuba Diving & Undersea walk

Bars, Pubs, Massages and Spa:

Bars & Pubs:Hooters, Wetherspoons , Blue sky III,The Irish Rovers restaurant , Club Insomnia, Ice bar V20 ,Green Tree Gulliver’s, Jazz Pitt & Hard Rock Cafe

Massages and Spa: 7 Spa Masha Luxury, Oasis Spa, Relief Spa, Avani Spa, Suksabai Massage, Grace Spa & The Relax Spa and massage.

4

Beaches

The beaches in Pattaya is all about azure water, white sand, romantic strolls, and the view of the shining city. The pristine beaches are one of the reasons to visit Pattaya with your wife.

Couples can enjoy a relaxed and romantic evening at the beaches. You can also relish the stunning view of the sparkling ocean from the shades of palm trees bordering the beach.
